Acknowledgement of Country
We acknowledge and pay respect to the Gadigal people of the Eora Nation who are the traditional custodians of the land on which this meeting takes place, and to Elders past and present. It is upon their ancestral land that the University of Sydney is built. As we share our knowledge, teaching, learning and research practices within this university may we also pay respect to the knowledge embedded forever within the Aboriginal Custodianship of Country
